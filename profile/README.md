---

## About

**Big Dope** is an international beatbox ecosystem combining **education, media, events and technology**.

Our mission is to build the most advanced **beatbox academy and creative platform** in the world.

We work at the intersection of:

- beatbox culture
- music technology
- education
- community development
- digital media

Big Dope is designed not just as a school — but as a **full ecosystem for beatbox artists**.

---

## Ecosystem

Big Dope consists of several core directions.

### 🎓 Big Dope Academy
Professional beatbox education platform.

Features:

- structured beatbox learning programs
- beginner → professional progression
- mentorship from top beatboxers
- breathing techniques and sound design
- music production integration

Future roadmap:

- online academy platform
- certification system
- verified instructors
- global student network

## Vision

Big Dope is building the operating layer for modern beatbox: education systems, media pipelines, creative tooling, and global community infrastructure.

Our long-term vision is to become the standard platform where beatbox learning, music technology, and creator growth connect in one ecosystem.

We focus on:

- scalable beatbox education infrastructure
- AI-powered music and vocal tooling
- creator-first media and battle platforms
- global collaboration between artists, students, and developers

---

## Repositories

This GitHub organization is structured as a multi-repository ecosystem, where each repository powers a specific part of the Big Dope platform.

- `academy-platform` — core academy product and learning infrastructure
- `beatbox-ai` — sound analysis, vocal ML, and assistive AI tools
- `battle-platform` — battle operations, formats, and competition workflows
- `content-tools` — media automation, publishing workflows, and pipeline tooling
- `internal-tools` — internal systems used by team and operations
